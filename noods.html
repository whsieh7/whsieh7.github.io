Hand Pulled Noodels

Also know as lamian which translates to "pull noodles", hand pulled noodles are made by twisting, stretching and folding the dough into strands. Each time the noodes are folded and stretched, the number doubles nad their width shrinks in half. The dough is a simple mixture of flour and water.
